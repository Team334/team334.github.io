Announcement of the 2019 Dozer Games!

January 4th 2019

This year a pre-kickoff tradition is born with the creation of the Dozer Games. The Dozer Games is a 24 hour competition leading up to the kickoff of the FRC game with different mini contests, quizzes, and events. The winner will be receiving the Dozer Games trophy and a prize (yes it has monetary value).

Find out some more about this FRC hero from the video below.
