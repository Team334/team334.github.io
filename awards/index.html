Awards

Since winning the Rookie All Start Award in 1999, the TechKnights have always strived for excellence. Competing for over 20 years in the FIRST Robotics Competition, they have received numerous awards for their work. The TechKnights have achieved a diverse range of successes, from winning the Chairman's Award in 2003 to the NYC Regional in 2014 to multiple Engineering Inspiration Awards along the way.
