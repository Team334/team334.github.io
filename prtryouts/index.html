You ready to join us?

Brooklyn Technical High School's Robotics Team has been an integral part of the school community since the team's founding.
It encompasses the school's spirit of engineering achievement and innovation, while fostering the development of young leaders.
Members are encouraged to take initiative under the guidance of seasoned coaches and mentors.

This year we are holding open tryouts for our newly restructered Public Relations division. We are looking for those with expertise in:

  • Photography
  • Videography
  • Writing
  • Fundraising/Sponsor Relations
  • Social Media
If you fit one of or all of these descriptions and are looking for a fun way to hone your skills and be on the bleeding edge of technological research and development fill out an application!


(Make sure you sign in with your BTHS email!)

Application requirements:

A detailed listing of every requirement is on the application itself.

General Requirements:

  • Must attend Brooklyn Technical High School as a current student as of 2017-2018 school year Applications for incoming freshman will be considered in the fall
  • Choice of position
    • Photographer
    • Writer (creative or copy)
    • Videographer
    • Fundraising/Sponsor Relations
    • Social Media Moderator
  • Free form sample
    • A file of any type that you would like to share with us! It can relate to who you are, what you like to do, or what you are applying for on the team. This is your oppertunity to show us what is unique about you!

Photographer Specific requirements:

  • Sports Shot
  • Candid Shot

Videographer Specific requirements:

  • Editing Sample
  • Outline

Writer Specific requirements:

  • Sample Press release
  • Chairman's Sample response

Fundraising/Sponsor Relations Specific requirements:

  • Irate sponsor response
